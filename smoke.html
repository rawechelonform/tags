Grab Final GAS URL

1) Choose a small image and click Open final URL.
2) The page will navigate. Copy the entire URL from the browser address bar — it will start with https://script.googleusercontent.com/macros/echo?user_content_key=...&lib=....
3) Hit Back to return here.

Once you have the long URL, paste it into the action="…" of your real form(s) and the normal smoke test.
